Our Story

Paletería Alegría Michoacana was founded with the vision of bringing authentic Mexican paletas to the community. Our journey began in a small town in Michoacán, where we learned the traditional methods of making paletas using fresh, natural ingredients. Today, we continue to honor those traditions while adding our own unique twist to create a variety of flavors that delight our customers.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality, delicious paletas that bring joy to our customers. We are committed to using only the finest ingredients and to creating a welcoming environment where everyone can enjoy our treats. Whether you're looking for a refreshing snack on a hot day or a sweet treat to share with friends and family, Paletería Alegría Michoacana is here to serve you.
